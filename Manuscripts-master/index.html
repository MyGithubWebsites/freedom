Quinoa Salad

Quinoa is a healthy, nutrient-dense food that provides a wide variety of minerals, vitamins, and antioxidants. It's also high in fiber and contains all nine essential amino acids, making it a complete protein source.

Dry Fruits

Dry fruits are rich in proteins, calcium, and other micronutrients which help to maintain healthy bones and strengthen them. Dry fruits are very effective in the battle against depression and stress. They also improve brain health and memory.

Broccoli

Broccoli is a good source of fibre and protein, and contains iron, potassium, calcium, selenium and magnesium as well as the vitamins A, C, E, K and a good array of B vitamins including folic acid.
